About us:

Los Studios Inc. is a music production company, that offers recording, mixing and mastering services in Lieto, Finland.

Our operation is mainly based on music production, but we also offer professional marketing services.

In addition to our own productions we continuously work with a wide variety of different kinds of bands and artists

Customers can also order productions from us to all sorts of projects.

The Studio:

Our modern studio guarantees a professional quality for your recordings, without any unwanted echoes or hums.

We think the quality of air is paramount when spending long periods of time inside.

This is why we have a recently installed a new Panasonic heat pump to keep the air fresh and clean to ensure our clients comfort on the longer recording sessions.
